The story

The person behind the lens

Since 2019, Samuel Long Productions has provided high-quality visual storytelling through video, photo, and aerial imaging. With over seven years of hands-on experience capturing weddings, sporting events, and creative commercial projects, I specialize in turning real moments into cinematic visuals that connect with your audience.

As an FAA-certified drone pilot and seasoned videographer, I bring both technical precision and artistic vision to every shoot. Whether filming on the ground or in the air, my goal is always the same: to deliver polished, emotionally resonant content tailored to your needs.

From intimate events to large-scale productions, I approach each project with professionalism, creativity, and an eye for detail — helping you preserve and share your story with clarity and impact.
